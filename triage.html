TRIAGE™

Decision framework behind
every venture we build.

A proprietary assessment framework that evaluates medical technologies across regulatory, clinical, capital, and market dimensions as one interdependent system.

How It Works

Integrated dimensions.
One decision architecture.

The regulatory pathway determines clinical evidence requirements. Evidence design determines reimbursement viability. Reimbursement determines capital logic. TRIAGE™ maps these dependencies before any are executed in isolation.

Scientific & Clinical Evidence

Evidence strength, study design, endpoint alignment - mapped to regulatory demands and designed to support reimbursement claims simultaneously.

Regulatory Authorization

MDR classification, Notified Body strategy, conformity assessment pathway - integrated with clinical evidence from Day One.

Market Access & Adoption

Reimbursement landscape, payer segmentation, health economics - aligned to the regulatory pathway and clinical evidence.

Capital Strategy

Funding architecture, deployment timing, non-dilutive instruments, governed by the risk profile of all other dimensions.

Why It Works

Designed as one system.
Executed as one system.

The industry's operating model evaluates each dimension in isolation. A regulatory consultant assesses classification. Separately, a clinical advisor designs evidence. Independently, a market access firm models reimbursement. The result: strategies that contradict each other, timelines that compound, and capital consumed by re-work.

TRIAGE™ reverses this by forcing synchronization from Day One. Every strategic decision is stress-tested against every other dimension before execution begins. The equity model guarantees it: Siaxen's return depends entirely on the architecture producing an investable venture.

What You Gain

What TRIAGE™ produces.

Structural Coherence

Regulatory, clinical, market, and capital strategies that reinforce rather than contradict each other. Every pathway verified against every other.

Risk Architecture

Every structural risk identified, characterized, and mapped before capital is committed. No surprises from unexamined dependencies.

Investment Readiness

A venture that reaches the investment table with structured assumptions, disciplined capital logic, and the coherence investors demand.
