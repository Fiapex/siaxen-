About Us

We build MedTech ventures
the way they should be built.

Siaxen co-founds MedTech companies from patented science and takes full operational responsibility for their path to market. Strategy, capital, and execution designed together and led together.

Why We Exist

Thousands of medical technologies.
A fraction reach patients.

Each year, Western European produce thousands of patented medical technologies, most of which never reach the market. The science is not the problem. What fails is the commercialization architecture around it: fragmented advisory, disconnected regulatory and capital strategies, each designed in isolation.

Siaxen exists to close that gap. We provide the integrated commercialization system and co-founder accountability that turns validated science into funded, scalable companies.

Where We Operate

At the center of the European
life sciences corridor.

Siaxen operates from Brussels, Belgium, adjacent to the EU regulatory institutions that shape medical device policy across the continent. The location places us within direct reach of the densest concentration of university technology transfer offices in Western Europe, and at the intersection where regulatory decisions, clinical standards, and reimbursement frameworks converge.
